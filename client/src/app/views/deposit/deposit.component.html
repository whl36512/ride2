Deposit Money

Deposit and withdraw are done through the Zelle network, offered by many banks, such as Chase Bank's QuickPay with Zelle, as well as Bank of America, US Bank, PNC Bank , and other major banks.

Generally it takes a few seconds for our bank to notify us the deposit.

Your Deposit Identification Number is

RD{{user_from_db.deposit_id}}

It will identify who sends money to us.

To deposit money into your account,

  1. send money to
    {{Constants.EMAIL_DEPOSIT}}
    through your bank and
  2. you MUST put your Deposit Identification Number in the memo field when you set up your deposit at your bank.
Once we receive your deposit, it will be reflected in your account balance.
To book a trip, you must have a positive balance to cover the cost of the trip.
